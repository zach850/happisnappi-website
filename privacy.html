Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Happi Snappi LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Niblio mobile application. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Niblio is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide the app's functionality:

  • Flashcard Content: The flashcards and study materials you create are stored locally on your device.
  • Study Progress: Your study statistics and progress data are stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, transaction data is processed by Apple and is subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Data Storage

All user-created content (flashcards, decks, study progress) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your study data to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Niblio uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Niblio does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is a general-purpose study tool and does not target children specifically.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Since your data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to it.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete your data at any time by deleting flashcard decks within the app or by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at z@happisnappi.com.
