Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Happi Snappi LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Niblio mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Niblio is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide the App's functionality:

  • Flashcard Content: The flashcards and study materials you create are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this content.
  • Study Progress: Your study statistics and progress data are stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, transaction data is processed entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not collect or store your payment information.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, location, or device identifiers through the App.

How We Use Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not use your data for any purpose. The App functions entirely on-device without transmitting data to external servers.

Data Storage and Security

All user-created content (flashcards, decks, study progress) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS data storage mechanisms. We do not transmit, process, or store your data on any external servers. Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode and encryption.

Data Retention

Your data persists on your device for as long as the App is installed. We do not retain any user data on our systems because we do not collect it.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Since your data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to it.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchase processing. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual flashcard decks within the App.
  • Uninstalling the App, which removes all associated data from your device.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). The App does not require account creation or collect any personal data. If you believe a child has somehow provided us with personal information, please contact us at z@happisnappi.com and we will take steps to address the issue.

Your Rights Under California Law (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information.

Since we do not collect, store, or sell personal information, these rights are satisfied by default. If you have questions, contact us at z@happisnappi.com.

International Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or other regions with data protection laws, please note that we do not collect or process your personal data. All App data remains on your device. You retain full control over your data and can delete it at any time as described above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
