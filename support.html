Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a flashcard deck?

Open Niblio and tap the "+" button to create a new deck. Give it a name, then add cards with a front (question) and back (answer).

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings on your device, tap your name, then Subscriptions. You can view, change, or cancel your Niblio subscription there.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed through Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow Apple's instructions.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete individual decks within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data.

Is my data backed up?

Your Niblio data is included in your device's iCloud or local backups if you have those enabled. We do not store your data on separate servers.

How do I restore purchases?

Open Niblio, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." This will restore any previous in-app purchases tied to your Apple ID.

The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app, or restarting your device. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed from the App Store. If you're still experiencing problems, contact us at z@happisnappi.com with a description of the issue.
